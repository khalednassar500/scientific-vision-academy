About Us

Welcome to Scientific Vision Academy, your premier destination for learning Arabic as a non-native speaker.


Scientific Vision Academy is a premier language learning institution based in the heart of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, in the vibrant city of Makkah, Al-Naseem. Our mission is to provide students from all over the world with the opportunity to learn Arabic in an immersive and engaging environment, led by experienced instructors who are passionate about teaching and sharing their knowledge.


At Scientific Vision Academy, we're passionate about sharing our love for the Arabic language with students from all over the world.


At Scientific Vision Academy, we believe that learning a new language is not just about mastering grammar and vocabulary, but also about gaining a deeper understanding of the culture and traditions that shape it. That's why we offer a comprehensive curriculum that combines language instruction with cultural insights and practical skills, designed to help you navigate real-life situations with confidence and ease.


Our experienced instructors are dedicated to helping you master Arabic in an engaging and interactive way, with a focus on practical conversation skills and cultural insights.


Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest technology and resources to support your learning journey, whether you are a beginner or an advanced learner. From interactive classrooms and multimedia tools to personalized attention and guidance, we strive to create a supportive and inclusive learning community where you can thrive and achieve your goals.


Join us today and experience the Scientific Vision Academy difference. Our doors are open to learners of all ages, backgrounds, and levels of proficiency, and our team is ready to welcome you with warmth and hospitality. Contact us today to learn more about our programs and how we can help you achieve your language learning goals.
